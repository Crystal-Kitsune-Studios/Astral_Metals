# Astral_Metals
Astral Metals is a revival of the old Metallurgy mod in spirit, but with its own unique content and interesting metals that will feed your need to explore the world to discover them all.

We will be providing content for the Overworld, the Nether, the End, and have hooks to provide additional content as the mod progresses over time.
